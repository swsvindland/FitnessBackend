# Home Gym 4 Day
- Barbell
- Dumbbells
- Squat Rack
- Flat Bench
- Incline Bench

This is a workout designed for users of home gyms. It is a 12 week, 4 day a week workout. It is designed to be an upper lower workout. This is a great workout for intermediate to advanced lifters, if you are newer, check out the three day variation or Strength 1.

## Monday
### Upper

- Bench Press
- Overhead Press
- Pullup
- Row
- Dumbbell Curl
- Lateral Raise

## Tuesday
### Lower

- Squat
- RDl
- Glute Bridge
- Calf Raise (toes out)
- Calf Raise (toes in)

## Thursday
### Upper

- Incline Bench Press
- DB Overhead Press
- Chin Up
- Chest Supported DB Row
- Hammer Curl
- Reverse Fly

## Friday
### Lower

- Hip Thrust
- Sumo Deadlift
- Goblet Squat
- Calf Raise (toes out)
- Calf Raise (toes in)
